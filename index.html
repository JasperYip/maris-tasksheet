01 Key Specifications Glider Platform
Hull Length
2.5 m
Streamlined pressure hull optimised for low hydrodynamic drag.
Operating Depth
0–200 m
Deep-water profiling envelope for lake and coastal trials.
Buoyancy Drive
VBD module
Variable buoyancy device enabling controlled ascent and descent.
Pitch Control
P&R module
Internal mass-shifting pitch and roll system for attitude control.
Navigation
9-DOF IMU
Inertial measurement with Madgwick filtering for orientation estimation.
Sensing Suite
4 sensors
Sonar, Pressure, GPS, and inertial sensors integrated via I2C on a Raspberry Pi.
02 Highlights Engineering Focus
↕
Variable Buoyancy
A custom-engineered VBD system shifts internal volume to drive vertical motion through the water column without propulsion.
⚙
Custom Electronics
Bespoke PCB design for the pitch & roll control board, with onboard fault diagnostics and isolated power domains.
⬡
Waterjet Skeleton
Modular internal frame designed for rapid assembly, easy access, and structural rigidity under hydrostatic load.
◎
Real-Time Telemetry
Live operations dashboard for the build and test team — task tracking, assembly instructions, and packing checklists.
